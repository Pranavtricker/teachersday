For Mrs. Siddhartha

In words you plant a seed, A spark, a thought, a dream; Through stories, lessons, deeds, You guide our minds to gleam.

Your patience is a gentle stream, Your guidance, a steady hand; With every poem, every theme, You help us understand.

You nurture hearts and minds alike, Through laughter, tales, and care; Each reading, each essay we strike, Shows the love you always share.

Forever grateful, we will be, For the light you bring each day; Your wisdom shapes the best in me, And guides me on my way.
